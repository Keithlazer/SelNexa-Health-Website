Careers at SelNexa Health

We build practical, sovereign healthcare infrastructure that creates measurable impact. Our values are pragmatism, sovereignty, and impact.

Our Mission

Enable African healthcare providers to deliver faster, safer, and more resilient care through infrastructure-aware digital platforms.

Our Values

  • Pragmatism: Build what works in real clinical environments.
  • Sovereignty: Respect local ownership, governance, and context.
  • Impact: Prioritise outcomes that improve patient and clinician experience.

How We Work

Remote-friendly teams across Africa, cross-functional collaboration, and field-informed product development with healthcare partners.
