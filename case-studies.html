Bulawayo General Hospital

Context: High outpatient throughput and recurring power/connectivity interruptions slowed triage and records continuity.

Intervention: Offline-first registration, queue orchestration, and synchronized longitudinal records rollout for frontline departments.

Metric change: 31% faster triage-to-documentation cycle and reduced paper fallback events in monitored wards.

Timeframe: 14-week pilot window (Q1 2026).

Clinical quote: "Staff maintained continuity during outages without reverting to fragmented paper processes."

Evidence: Interim operations review, with independent verification checkpoint scheduled post-pilot close.

Procurement Optimization (South Africa)

Context: Pharmacy teams managed variable demand and frequent urgent reorder cycles that increased unit costs.

Intervention: Forecast-guided reorder thresholds, expiry monitoring alerts, and weekly replenishment workflow standardization.

Metric change: 22% fewer emergency stock requests and improved restock predictability for essential medicines.

Timeframe: 12-week implementation and monitoring cycle.

Operations quote: "Our team now plans refill windows proactively instead of reacting to late shortages."

Evidence: Facility procurement dashboard review with baseline-versus-current analysis.

Interoperable EHR Project

Context: Fragmented systems created duplicate clinical workups and inconsistent patient history visibility between teams.

Intervention: Unified patient record model with consent-aware access, audit trails, and standards-first interoperability pathways.

Metric change: Reduced duplicate test ordering in pilot workflows and improved record completeness during handoffs.

Timeframe: 16-week phased deployment across intake, pharmacy, and clinician documentation streams.

Clinical quote: "Handover quality improved because essential history followed the patient across workflows."

Evidence: Pilot data-quality and handover-audit review; formal publication pending independent assessment.

Analytics Pilot

Context: Operations leaders lacked timely visibility into throughput bottlenecks and pathway-level risk indicators.

Intervention: Live operational dashboard with alerts for readmission risk, queue delays, and escalation thresholds.

Metric change: Improved reporting timeliness and earlier risk flagging for facility command-center teams.

Timeframe: 10-week analytics enablement sprint plus continuous monitoring.

Leadership quote: "We shifted from retrospective reporting to daily operational decision support."
