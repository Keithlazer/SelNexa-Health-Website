Why invest in SelNexa Health?

SelNexa Health is a Zimbabwe-based healthcare innovation organization building a portfolio of practical health systems initiatives. The SelNexa Health Platform is our first product: an offline-first hospital management system that addresses administration, procurement, and records continuity in low-connectivity environments.

Market Opportunity in African Healthcare

Healthcare demand growth continues while provider capacity, interoperable records, and resilient supply chains remain constrained. SelNexa Health addresses these structural gaps through an organization-led portfolio, starting with the Platform.

Workforce Shortages

Clinician capacity is constrained by heavy administrative load and fragmented systems.

Fragmented Records

Disconnected patient histories increase duplicate tests, treatment delays, and risk.

Supply Chain Inefficiencies

Manual procurement drives stockouts and waste in high-pressure clinical environments.

Differentiation

Offline-first architecture, auditability, and AI automation tuned for African infrastructure realities.

Team

Leadership combines healthcare operations, enterprise engineering, and public-private deployment expertise across multiple African markets.

Milestones Achieved

  • 47 % wait-time reduction across pilot sites
  • 62 % admin cost reduction in validated workflows
  • $180k annualized savings from procurement optimization
  • Partnerships with ministries and private facilities in 12 countries

Funding Needs
