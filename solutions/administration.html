AI-Powered Administration For Frontline Care Teams

Automate patient registration, scheduling, and record management with workflows designed for constrained healthcare environments.

Core Features

Automated Patient Registration

Intelligent forms that guide patients through registration, pre-populate records, and flag duplicates in real-time.

Smart Appointment Scheduling

AI suggests optimal time slots, prevents double-booking, sends automatic reminders, and reduces no-shows.

Intelligent Record Organization

Auto-categorize and file patient notes, lab results, and medical history. Full-text search for instant access.

Workflow Analytics

Track admin KPIs: registration time, appointment turnover, error rates. Identify bottlenecks and optimize processes.

Key Benefits

Designed for Faster Triage-to-Documentation

Workflow orchestration reduces handoff lag between intake and clinical documentation.

Workflow Bottlenecks Reduced

Automation reduces repetitive manual tasks and rework across front-desk teams.

Reduced Paper Fallbacks

Digital intake and documentation reduce paper-heavy handoffs during outages.

Operational Accuracy at Scale

Standardized workflows maintain compliance-ready records while supporting growth to multi-site operations.
