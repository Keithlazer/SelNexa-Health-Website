Health Records Module For The SelNexa Health Platform

Secure, interoperable clinical records with offline access, consent controls, cross-facility continuity, and tamper-evident audit history.

Core Features

Tamper-Evident Records

Every access and update is logged for authenticity, traceability, and clinical accountability.

End-to-End Encryption

Military-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Only authorized users with patient permission can view records.

Universal Interoperability

Standardized HL7/FHIR formats enable seamless data exchange with any EHR or healthcare system. No proprietary silos.

Patient Consent Control

Patients grant/revoke access permissions in real time. Audit logs show exactly who accessed their data and when.

Key Benefits

Compliance-Ready Access Controls

Role-based controls and audit trails support policy-aligned records governance.

Resilient Continuity During Outages

Offline persistence and controlled sync reduce operational disruption during connectivity gaps.

Reduced Duplicate Workups

Unified records improve handoff visibility and reduce repeated diagnostics in pilot workflows.

Improved Cross-Facility Record Access

Consent-aware interoperability supports safer referral and transfer continuity.
