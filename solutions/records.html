Clinical Records

Core Features

Blockchain-Secured Records

Immutable, tamper-proof patient records using blockchain technology. Every transaction is logged and verified for authenticity.

End-to-End Encryption

Military-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Only authorized users with patient permission can view records.

Universal Interoperability

Standardized HL7/FHIR formats enable seamless data exchange with any EHR or healthcare system. No proprietary silos.

Patient Consent Control

Patients grant/revoke access permissions in real time. Audit logs show exactly who accessed their data and when.

Key Benefits

100% HIPAA Compliance

Full regulatory compliance with automated auditing, encryption, and access controls built in.

Zero Data Loss

Blockchain redundancy ensures patient records are never lost, corrupted, or deleted without authorization.

Cross-Facility Access

Providers across different hospitals instantly access complete patient history. No more requesting old records.

Privacy & Patient Trust
