About SelNexa Health

Pioneering healthcare technology solutions for Africa

Our Mission

SelNexa Health was founded to address the critical challenges facing healthcare delivery systems across Africa. We believe that technology, particularly AI and blockchain, can transform how healthcare is administered, making it more efficient, accessible, and effective.

Our Approach

We develop solutions specifically designed for the African healthcare context, considering infrastructure limitations, diverse patient populations, and unique administrative challenges.
