Telemedicine in Zimbabwe — SelNexa Health Platform

Remote consultations, secure records, and AI-powered triage are making quality care available beyond city centers.

Access to specialists and timely diagnosis remains a major challenge in many parts of Zimbabwe. SelNexa Health's telemedicine platform connects patients and clinicians using secure, low-bandwidth video, integrated patient records, and decision support tools.

Why telemedicine matters here

  • Reduces travel and waiting times for patients in rural areas.
  • Enables task-shifting and remote supervision for smaller clinics.
  • Supports chronic disease follow-up and medication adherence with remote monitoring.

Key features SelNexa provides

  1. Secure video consultations with low bandwidth fallbacks.
  2. Integrated EHR view for clinicians during the visit.
  3. AI-assisted triage to prioritize urgent cases.
  4. Appointment scheduling and reminders that link to clinicians' calendars.
