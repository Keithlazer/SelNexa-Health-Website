Appointment FAQs

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel or reschedule appointments up to 24 hours before. After that, a cancellation fee may apply. Contact support for special cases.

How do video consultations work?

You'll receive a secure video link via email. Join from your phone, tablet, or computer. Make sure you have a camera and microphone.

Is my information secure?

Yes. All appointments and health data are encrypted and HIPAA-compliant. Only authorized healthcare providers can access your information.

Can I bring someone with me?

Yes. For in-person appointments, you can bring a family member. For telemedicine, you can ask a caregiver to join the video call.
