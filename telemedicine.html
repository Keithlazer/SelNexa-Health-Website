Telemedicine built for Africa

"SelNexa’s telemedicine platform enables secure video consultations even on low‑bandwidth connections, integrates patient records and uses AI triage to prioritise care. In our Zimbabwe pilot, telemedicine reduced travel time by 85 %, saved $50–100 per consultation, increased specialist access by 300 % and improved treatment adherence by 40 %."

Secure Video Consultations

Consultations remain stable even on constrained 2G/3G links through adaptive bitrate fallback and session recovery. Every session uses end-to-end encryption and role-based controls to protect patient privacy.

Appointment Scheduling & Reminders

Calendar-linked booking synchronises with clinician availability and sends automated SMS or email reminders to reduce no-shows. Demo and telemedicine requests are unified in one scheduling flow.

Patient Portal & EHR Integration

Providers and patients access records in real time with consent-aware access controls. Encounter notes, vitals, and prescriptions synchronise back to SelNexa records for continuity of care.

AI-Assisted Triage

Machine learning classifies symptom urgency and routes high-risk patients to immediate follow-up. Clinicians receive priority queues so urgent cases are seen sooner.

Remote Monitoring & Chronic Care

Long-term care pathways support hypertension, diabetes, and maternal health cohorts with reminders, check-ins, and outcomes tracking. Offline support allows data capture during connectivity gaps.

Offline & Multilingual Support

Interfaces are optimised for multilingual teams and can operate offline-first in low-connectivity environments. Translation support includes English, Shona, and Ndebele with progressive rollout for more languages.
