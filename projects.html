SELNEXA HEALTH PROJECT PORTFOLIO

One organization, multiple health systems initiatives

SelNexa Health builds, funds, and deploys practical healthcare systems for Africa. The SelNexa Health Platform is our first active product, and future projects extend the same mission into reproductive health, maternal care, and community health infrastructure.

Current And Upcoming Projects

Each project is part of SelNexa Health's broader work to strengthen African healthcare infrastructure.

PROJECT 01 · ACTIVE

SelNexa Health Platform

An offline-first hospital management system for registration, records, procurement, billing, analytics, and operational continuity in low-connectivity environments.

Role in the portfolio: The flagship platform proving SelNexa Health's infrastructure-aware design approach.

ContraWise

A reproductive health access initiative focused on private, stigma-free guidance, clinic discovery, and continuity for women navigating contraception decisions.

Maternal And Neonatal Health Access

A pathway initiative for early-warning workflows, referral coordination, facility readiness, and safer maternal handoffs across constrained systems.

How We Decide What To Build

Infrastructure Fit

Projects must work under local power, connectivity, staffing, and governance realities.

Measurable Impact

Each initiative needs a clear baseline, pilot metric, and path to independent evaluation.

Sustainable Deployment

We prioritize systems that can be funded, maintained, and owned with African healthcare partners.
