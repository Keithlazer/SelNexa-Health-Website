PROJECT 01 · SELNEXA HEALTH PLATFORM

Offline-first hospital management for African care teams

The SelNexa Health Platform is the flagship product from SelNexa Health. It keeps registration, records, procurement, billing, and reporting available when power or connectivity fails.

Explore All Projects

Platform Modules

These are capabilities of the SelNexa Health Platform, not separate company identities.

AI-driven administration

The Platform automates registration, scheduling, claims, and reporting so clinical teams spend less time on coordination work and more time with patients.

  • Offline-capable patient intake and queue flow
  • Scheduling and billing workflows tuned to local rules
  • Operational dashboards for administrators
Explore administration workflows →

Predictive procurement

The Platform uses demand signals, seasonality, and facility history to help pharmacy teams prevent stock-outs without over-ordering medicines that may expire.

  • Forecast-guided reorder thresholds
  • Expiry and urgent-stock risk alerts
  • Procurement reviews designed for constrained budgets
Explore procurement workflows →

Offline-capable electronic health records

The Platform keeps patient histories accessible at the point of care, records actions with tamper-evident audit trails, and syncs securely when networks return.

  • Local data persistence during outages
  • Consent-aware access controls and auditability
  • FHIR-ready interoperability pathways
