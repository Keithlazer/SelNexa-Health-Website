Our Features

SelNexa Health’s platform is built on global best practices, including transparent data management and secure, HIPAA-compliant patient portals. All features are designed for accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), security (SSL certification), and interoperability with national systems. Key performance indicators (KPIs) are tracked and reported in line with WHO standards.

Real-time Data Access

Stay updated with live patient records, diagnostics, and care notes, ensuring seamless collaboration across departments.

Integrated Health Dashboards

Monitor KPIs such as patient outcomes, service efficiency, and equity of access. Dashboards are customizable and support public reporting in line with WHO standards.

Mobile-First Design

Access and manage health information on-the-go with our responsive and intuitive mobile interface.

Secure Patient Communication

All communications are encrypted and compliant with international privacy standards. Patient engagement tools are co-designed with local communities.

Offline Functionality

Capture and sync data even in areas with limited connectivity — ideal for rural health centers and outreach programs.

Analytics & Reporting Tools

Generate comprehensive reports for better decision-making, funding requests, and operational transparency.
