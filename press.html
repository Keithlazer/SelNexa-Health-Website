Press resources for SelNexa Health

SelNexa Health is a Zimbabwe-based youth-led health systems innovation organization building offline-first hospital management infrastructure for African clinics and hospitals.

Company Boilerplate

SelNexa Health builds practical digital infrastructure for African healthcare systems. Its flagship product, the SelNexa Health Platform, is an offline-first hospital management system designed for clinics and hospitals operating with unstable power, low bandwidth, and local data governance requirements.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 2024
  • Headquarters: Harare, Zimbabwe
  • Flagship product: SelNexa Health Platform
  • Target markets: Zimbabwe, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa
  • Stage: active pilot deployment and market conversations

Founder Bios

Keith Tafangombe

Founder and CEO of SelNexa Health. Keith leads the organization's product strategy, health systems architecture, and deployment partnerships for resilient African healthcare infrastructure.

Guide Chitewe

Chief Technology Officer. Guide leads offline-first architecture, low-bandwidth optimization, and AI infrastructure for the SelNexa Health Platform.
