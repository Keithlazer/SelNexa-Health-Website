Building African Healthcare's Operating System

SelNexa Health orchestrates hospital administration, supply chains, and patient records engineered to operate reliably across low bandwidth infrastructure where most African healthcare happens. We combine cutting edge technology with deep understanding of local realities.

Our Strategic Vision

Our Mission

Orchestrate hospital operations, supply chains, and patient records into an integrated intelligence layer that operates reliably across African infrastructure constraints—so healthcare providers can focus on what matters: patient care.

Our Thesis

African healthcare is not replicating Western healthcare IT. It's defining its own architecture, one engineered for offline first operation, low bandwidth resilience, and local data sovereignty. We're building the platform for that transformation.

Our Values

Pragmatism: Technology that works in reality, not just in theory. Sovereignty: Data stays local; decisions stay with healthcare leaders. Impact: Every feature must measurably improve operations or patient outcomes.

Pilot Efficiency Report — Hardened Validation

Measured outcomes from our infrastructure‑hostile deployments

Achieved 100% data retention during internet outages across four pilot facilities; realized a 47% reduction in patient wait times and a 62% reduction in administrative processing costs in operational workflows—measured across a 90‑day hardened validation in infrastructure‑hostile conditions.

Why Offline‑First: SelNexa’s offline‑first design ensures clinical operations continue uninterrupted when connectivity or power fail. Patient records and critical workflows sync reliably when networks return, so care teams never lose access to the information they need—prioritizing reliability and uptime over fleeting feature claims.
