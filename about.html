Our Mission

Our mission is to empower healthcare providers and patients through solutions that are rigorously evaluated and transparently reported. We adhere to WHO’s Guidance for Best Practices for Clinical Trials and commit to public disclosure of all research results. Our work is measured by robust monitoring and evaluation frameworks, with KPIs including patient outcomes, system efficiency, and equity of access.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, SelNexa Health embeds research and technology within national health infrastructure, co-designing solutions with local stakeholders and communities. Our work is guided by global standards and local priorities, ensuring sustainable, scalable impact. We actively engage with patients, communities, and national health systems from project inception through implementation and evaluation.
