ABOUT SELNEXA HEALTH

A youth-led organization building resilient health systems for Africa

SelNexa Health is a Zimbabwe-based health systems innovation organization. We build, fund, and deploy practical digital infrastructure for African clinics and hospitals, starting with our flagship product, the SelNexa Health Platform.

Our Mission And Vision

Our Mission

Close Africa's healthcare infrastructure gap by building sustainable systems that work in the environments where care is actually delivered.

Our Thesis

African healthcare will not be strengthened by importing fragile tools built for stable infrastructure. SelNexa Health designs from local constraints outward: offline continuity, low bandwidth resilience, local governance, and practical implementation.

Our Values

Pragmatism: Build what can survive field conditions. Sovereignty: Keep data governance close to local health leaders. Impact: Measure whether each initiative improves care, operations, or access.

Pilot Efficiency Report — Hardened Validation

Measured outcomes from our infrastructure‑hostile deployments

The SelNexa Health Platform has sustained offline continuity across monitored outages, with pilot tracking showing a 31% faster triage-to-documentation cycle and 22% fewer emergency stock requests in the most recent cohort review (Q1 2026).

Why Offline-First: The Platform's offline-first design ensures clinical operations continue uninterrupted when connectivity or power fail. Patient records and critical workflows sync reliably when networks return, so care teams never lose access to the information they need.
