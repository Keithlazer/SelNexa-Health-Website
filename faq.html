FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About SelNexa

Find answers to common questions about our AI diagnostics, blockchain EHR, and healthcare solutions.

How long does implementation take? ▼

Most healthcare facilities can be fully operational within 2 weeks. Our implementation team handles system integration, staff training, and data migration. The timeline depends on your facility's size and existing infrastructure.

What about data security and privacy? ▼

We use enterprise-grade encryption and blockchain technology to secure patient data. All data is HIPAA compliant, and patients have complete control over their medical information. We undergo regular security audits and comply with international healthcare data standards.

Does it work without internet connection? ▼

Yes! SelNexa is designed to work offline. All critical functions operate without internet, and data automatically syncs when connection is restored. This is essential for healthcare facilities in remote areas with unreliable connectivity.

What's the pricing model? ▼

We offer flexible pricing based on facility size and patient volume. Most facilities see ROI within 6 months through cost savings and improved efficiency. We also offer a free 30-day trial with no credit card required.

What training and support do you provide? ▼

We provide comprehensive training for all staff members, 24/7 local support in your timezone, and continuous updates. Our support team is available via phone, email, and chat to help with any questions or issues.

Can it integrate with existing systems? ▼

Yes, SelNexa integrates seamlessly with existing healthcare systems using FHIR standards. Our team handles all integration work, ensuring minimal disruption to your current operations.
