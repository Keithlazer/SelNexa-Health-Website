Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about SelNexa Health? We've got answers.

General Questions

What is SelNexa Health?

SelNexa Health is an AI-powered platform designed to transform healthcare delivery in resource-constrained settings. We offer integrated solutions across administration, procurement, clinical records, analytics, and telemedicine—helping healthcare facilities improve patient outcomes, reduce costs, and eliminate errors.

Who is SelNexa Health for?

Our platform is built for healthcare facilities of all sizes—from small clinics to hospital networks. We're especially focused on serving healthcare systems in Africa and other developing markets where access to reliable infrastructure and advanced healthcare technology is critical.

How long has SelNexa Health been operating?

SelNexa Health is currently in beta, working with pilot facilities across multiple countries to refine our platform based on real-world healthcare workflows and needs. We're actively recruiting healthcare partners for our beta program.

Is SelNexa Health open source?

SelNexa Health is proprietary software designed to meet the stringent requirements of healthcare delivery. However, we are committed to transparency and compliance. We partner with open-source health IT standards (HL7, FHIR) to ensure interoperability.

Security & Compliance

Is SelNexa Health HIPAA compliant?

Yes. SelNexa Health is designed to meet HIPAA requirements for healthcare data protection in US facilities. For international deployments, we comply with equivalent regulations (GDPR in EU, POPIA in South Africa, etc.). All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit using military-grade encryption.

How is patient data encrypted?

SelNexa Health uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Patient records are additionally secured by blockchain technology, creating an immutable audit trail. Only authorized users with explicit patient consent can access records.

What happens if there's a data breach?

SelNexa Health maintains comprehensive incident response protocols and cyber insurance. In the event of any security incident, we notify affected users immediately, conduct a full audit, and provide support as required by law. Our blockchain audit logs ensure we can always account for who accessed what data and when.

Can patients control who accesses their data?

Absolutely. SelNexa Health gives patients granular control over their records. They can grant or revoke access to specific providers, departments, or facilities in real time. Every access is logged and patients can review their audit trail at any time.

Technical & Integration

Will SelNexa Health work with our existing EHR system?

Yes. SelNexa Health is built on HL7/FHIR standards for healthcare data interchange. We can integrate with most modern EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, OpenMRS, Bahmni, etc.). Our team will work with your IT department to ensure smooth integration with minimal disruption.

What internet speed do I need?

SelNexa Health is optimized for low-bandwidth environments. Core features work on 1 Mbps connections. Video telemedicine requires 2.5 Mbps upstream and downstream. We also offer offline-first modes so providers can work without connectivity and sync data when back online.

Can we deploy SelNexa Health on-premise?

Yes. SelNexa Health offers both cloud and on-premise deployment options. On-premise deployments are ideal for facilities with strict data residency requirements or limited internet connectivity. Our team manages setup and ongoing maintenance.

What devices does SelNexa Health support?

SelNexa Health is mobile-first and works on Android phones, iPhones, tablets, and computers. Our web app works in any modern browser. We support older devices and lower-bandwidth connections to ensure access across diverse healthcare settings.

Implementation & Support

How long does implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 6-12 weeks depending on facility size, existing systems, and integration complexity. This includes data migration, staff training, testing, and go-live support. We work closely with your team to minimize disruption to operations.

What training does my team get?

We provide comprehensive training: documentation, video tutorials, in-person workshops for key staff, and ongoing support. Every facility gets a dedicated success manager who can answer questions and optimize workflows for your specific use case.

What support is available after launch?

SelNexa Health provides 24/7 technical support via email, chat, and phone. Our support team has deep healthcare domain knowledge and can help troubleshoot issues, answer questions, and suggest improvements based on your usage patterns.

Do you offer customization for our facility's workflows?

Yes. While SelNexa Health provides a core platform with best-practice workflows, we can customize features to match your facility's specific processes. Custom development is available for larger deployments or unique use cases.

Pricing & Licensing

What is the pricing model?

SelNexa Health offers flexible licensing models: per-user monthly subscriptions, facility-wide subscriptions, or outcome-based pricing (pay based on cost savings achieved). We also offer special pricing for academic institutions, NGOs, and government facilities. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

Is there a free trial or pilot program?

Yes! We have an active beta program for qualified healthcare facilities. Pilot deployments typically run for 3-6 months at reduced or no cost. We're looking for healthcare partners who want to help shape the future of healthcare technology in emerging markets.

What's included in the subscription?

All SelNexa Health subscriptions include: platform access, data storage, software updates, security patches, user support, and basic training. Premium tiers add advanced analytics, custom integrations, and dedicated account management.

Can we start small and scale up later?

Absolutely. Many facilities start with one module (e.g., telemedicine) and add more (administration, procurement, analytics) over time. Our pricing scales with your usage, so you only pay for what you use.

Results & Outcomes

What kind of ROI can we expect?

Our clients typically see measurable returns within 3-6 months. Average outcomes: 40% cost reduction (especially in procurement), 50% reduction in administrative time, 70% fewer medical errors, 25% improvement in patient satisfaction. Long-term benefits compound as your team optimizes workflows.
