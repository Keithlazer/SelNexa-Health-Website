Back SelNexa Health's portfolio of African healthcare initiatives

SelNexa Health is a Zimbabwe-based organization building sustainable healthcare systems for Africa. Our first initiative, the SelNexa Health Platform, is an offline-first hospital management product; future projects extend the same mission into reproductive health, maternal care, and community infrastructure.

Why Fund SelNexa Health

Systems-Level Need

African clinics and hospitals need resilient infrastructure that works despite power, connectivity, staffing, and supply-chain constraints.

Focused First Product

The SelNexa Health Platform gives funders a measurable first initiative: offline-first hospital operations with pilot metrics and evaluation targets.

African-Led Team

SelNexa Health is led from Zimbabwe by builders who design around local clinical, operational, and infrastructure realities.

Infrastructure-Aware Technology

Offline-first architecture, low-bandwidth syncing, local data governance, and practical implementation support are built into the Platform.

Sustainable Model

Project funding supports pilot hardening while recurring platform deployments create a path toward durable operations.

Broader Portfolio

Future projects in reproductive health, maternal referral pathways, and community health infrastructure make SelNexa Health more than a single-product effort.
